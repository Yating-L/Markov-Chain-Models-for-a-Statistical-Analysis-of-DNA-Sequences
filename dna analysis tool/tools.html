Motif Selection

Motif select problem is to select important motifs from a large amount of motifs that output from motif discovery tools. Motif selection narrows down the number of motifs from motif discovery tools can enhance the accuracy and precision of motif discovery methods.

Three algorithms, greedy, integer linear programming, and tabu search, are used for motif selection.
